A missing AGI bottleneck:

governability after distribution

Once capable AI is cheap, local, and forkable, safety cannot depend only on provider moderation or hosted APIs.

It needs a bounded execution layer where:
- AI may propose
- actions are receipted
- contradictions stay visible
- projection is not truth
- only humans accept canonical state

I’ve published a working paper on this: The Sovereign Acceptance Runtime.

This is not a finished-platform claim.
It is a doctrine paper on the missing governance layer for portable AI.
